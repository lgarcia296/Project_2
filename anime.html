My Favorite Anime

I first started watching anime during Covid-19, and since then, I've watched over 30 series. Here are my top 3 favorites:

#3: Naruto

Naruto captivated me with its story and fight scenes, making it my third favorite.

#2: Bleach

Bleach is my second favorite because of its engaging sword-fighting action and beautiful animation.

#1: Yu-Gi-Oh

Yu-Gi-Oh is my top favorite because it introduced me to the world of trading card games, a passion that has stayed with me.
