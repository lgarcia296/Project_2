My Favorite Anime

I first started watching anime during Covid-19, and since then, I’ve watched over 30 series. Here are my top 3 favorites.

#3 Naruto

Naruto ranks third on my list for its intense fights and the inspiring story. It introduced me to many other anime.

#2 Bleach

Bleach is second, with thrilling sword fights and animation that keep me engaged every time I watch it.

#1 Yu-Gi-Oh!

Yu-Gi-Oh! is my all-time favorite because it’s been with me since fourth grade. I still collect cards, with over 100 displayed in my room.
