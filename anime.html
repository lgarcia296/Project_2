My Favorite Anime

I first started watching anime during Covid-19, and ever since that time, I've watched more than 30 anime series. Here are my top 3 favorites.

#3 Naruto

Naruto is one of my favorite anime that I've watched, and it's in my third spot because I enjoy watching every fight that appeared and the story it told. It also led me to find many similar animes that I now enjoy watching.

#2 Bleach

Bleach is an anime that has a very interesting story that I enjoy since it's pretty much sword fighting. That is why it's on my second spot for my list; the fights and animation for Bleach are something I enjoy watching.

#1 Yu-Gi-Oh

On my number one spot, I choose Yu-Gi-Oh since I started to watch and play it when I was in 4th grade. Ever since I started to watch Yu-Gi-Oh, I never forgot about it, and I still buy packs to play with the cards. Even to this day, I still have over 100 cards in my room as a precious memory that I will always keep.
